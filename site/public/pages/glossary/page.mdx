### BEV - Battery Electric Vehicle

A vehicle that is 100% electric and uses a battery of some sort to store power. They are also a lot of fun to drive. Read more at [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battery_electric_vehicle)

### FCEV - Fuel Cell Electric Vehicle

A vehicle thatis powered by a some fuel cell. Fuel cells tend to use hydrogen as the fuel of choice and convert the hydrogen into electricity which then powers a motor. Unfortunately
producing hydrogen is not very energy efficient at this time.
